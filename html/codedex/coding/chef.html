Margherita Pizza Recipe

  • 1 pizza base
  • 200 grams of natural tomato sauce
  • 200 grams of fresh mozzarella
  • A few fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat the oven to 250°C.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce over the pizza base.
  3. Slice the mozzarella and place it on top of the tomato.
  4. Add salt and pepper.
  5. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
  6. Upon removing from the oven, add the basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil.
